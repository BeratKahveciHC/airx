## Data-Driven Decision Making in HR

Saying "absences increased last month" is easy. But in which department? On which days? In which employee profile? Decisions made without answers to these questions are usually not the right ones.

Business Intelligence (BI) modules exist precisely to fill this gap.

## What Data Can You Analyze?

### Absence Analysis
- Absence rates by department
- Weekly/monthly trend charts
- Periods with the highest absence
- Distribution of sick leave vs. unpaid leave

### Overtime Tracking
- Overtime hours by person and department
- Budget comparison
- Unusual overtime alerts

### Check-In/Check-Out Analysis
- Average arrival times
- Early/late departure rates
- Location-based distribution (for multi-site organizations)

## How Does It Change the Decision-Making Process?

**Example scenario:** An HR manager feels that "Monday absences are high" but can't prove it.

With the BI module, a 30-second query reveals the Monday absence rate over the past 6 months, a comparison with other days, and which departments it's concentrated in. Now it's not intuition talking — it's data.
