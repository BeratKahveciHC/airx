## The Problems with Traditional Attendance Systems

Classic personnel attendance control systems rely on physical hardware: fingerprint scanners, card readers, turnstile systems. These devices come with:

- **High installation costs**
- **Ongoing maintenance expenses**
- **Risk of failure** — when one breaks, the entire system stops
- **Questionable KVKK compliance** (biometric data)

## The Rise of Mobile QR

In Turkey, where smartphone penetration exceeds 90%, the vast majority of employees carry a phone in their pocket. Turning that phone into an attendance terminal offers major advantages in both cost and practicality.

**How does it work?**

1. The employee arrives at the workplace
2. They open the AiRX mobile app
3. They scan the QR code at the entrance (or use location-based check-in)
4. The system automatically records the check-in time

## Comparison

| Feature | Traditional | Mobile QR |
|---|---|---|
| Hardware cost | High | Zero |
| Setup time | Weeks | 1 day |
| KVKK compliance | Risky | Fully compliant |
| Risk of failure | Yes | No |
| Remote work support | Not supported | Supported |

## Conclusion

The hardware-free attendance trend is accelerating. Organizations are switching to mobile-based systems to both cut costs and eliminate KVKK risk.
