## Cafeteria and HR: The Cost of Separate Systems

Many organizations use a separate software product for cafeteria management. This separation leads to the following problems:

- Two separate license costs
- Entering personnel changes into two systems separately
- Inconsistencies in entitlement calculations
- Reporting complexity

## The Advantages of Integrated Management

When the cafeteria module is integrated with the HR system:

**Personnel management is automatic:** A new hire is automatically added to the cafeteria list. An employee who leaves is automatically removed. No manual updates needed.

**Entitlement tracking is real-time:** Who ate on which day, which menu they chose — all of it is recorded. Month-end entitlement calculations are done automatically.

**Cost analysis:** Per-person meal cost, department-level spending, monthly trends — all of this data can be analyzed with the Business Intelligence module.
