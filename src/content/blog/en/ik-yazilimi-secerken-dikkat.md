## Why Is Choosing HR Software So Difficult?

There are dozens of HR software products on the market. They all promise similar features and all claim to be "easy to use." So which one is actually right for you?

Asking the right questions is half the battle in making the right decision.

## 1. Does It Collect Biometric Data?

Under KVKK, biometric data is classified as sensitive personal data. Systems that require fingerprint scanners carry serious legal risk. Ask: **"Does it work without biometric data?"**

## 2. How Long Does Setup Take?

Some systems require weeks of implementation — hardware orders, IT integrations, training sessions... The answer to **"How many days until we can start using it?"** should be 1–2 business days.

## 3. Is Support Available in Your Language?

When something goes wrong, you don't want to get lost in an English-language support portal. **A local support team** should be made up of people who both speak the language and understand local regulations.

## 4. What's Included in the Price?

Solutions sold under a "basic package" may charge separately for every feature you actually need. Ask: **"Are all 14 modules included in a single subscription?"**

## 5. Does the Mobile App Really Work?

Mobile apps that look great in a demo can become unusable in the field. Research real user reviews and run a pilot test if possible.

## 6. Where Is Your Data Stored?

Data stored on overseas servers may create additional obligations under KVKK. The question **"Is our data stored in Turkey?"** is critical.

## 7. Is It Scalable?

You have a team of 50 today, but you could have 500 in two years. The system should be able to grow with you, without requiring a reinstallation.

## Checklist

- [ ] No biometric data
- [ ] Setup in 1–2 days
- [ ] Local-language support team
- [ ] All modules in one subscription
- [ ] Local data center
- [ ] Scalable architecture
