## The Hidden Cost of Leave Management

On average, how many days does it take to get a leave request approved? In most organizations, the process looks like this:

1. The employee sends an email
2. The manager sees it but forgets
3. The employee follows up
4. The manager approves and notifies HR
5. HR enters it into the system

This process takes an average of **3–5 business days**. And at every step, there's a risk of error, forgetfulness, or something getting lost.

## How Does Automation Transform It?

In an automated leave process, the employee simply submits a request. The system handles the rest:

- Instant notification to the manager
- One-click approve/reject
- Automatic HR record entry
- Automatic notification to the employee
- Integration with timesheets

Total time: **5 minutes**.
